Simple Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Just your simple but great PB&J

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 Slices Sandwich Bread
  • 2 Tablespoons Peanut Butter
  • 2 Teaspoons Grape Jelly

DIRECTIONS

  1. Spread the peanut butter on one piece of bread.
  2. Spread the jelly on the other side.
  3. Put the two piece of bread together to form a sandwich.
